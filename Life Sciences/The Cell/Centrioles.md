# Centrioles
Centrioles are necessary for [[Mitosis]] in animal cells. All animal cells have two tiny centrioles that produce spindle fibres to pull apart the chromosomes into chromatids. They are cylindrical tubes that sit at 90Â° to each other until they separate during cell division.
