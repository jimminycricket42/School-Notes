# Speed 
**Definition**: The rate of change of distance. ^definition

**Unit of Measure**: $m.s^{-1}$

$$\begin{gather}
\\& v_{average} = \frac{s}{\Delta t}
\\
\\& Where:
\\& v_{average} = \textrm{Average Speed } (m.s^{-1})
\\& s = \textrm{metres } (m)
\\& t = \textrm{time } (s)
\end{gather}$$
