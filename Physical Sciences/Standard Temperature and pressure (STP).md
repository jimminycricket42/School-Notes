# Standard Temperature and Pressure
#terminology #physics/particles/gases 

## Standard Conditions:
pressure = 101,3 kPa
Temperature = 273 °K (0°c)

1 mole of (any) gas under standard conditions will have a volume of 22,4dm<sup>3</sup>
