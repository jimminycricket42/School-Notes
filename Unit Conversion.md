# Unit Conversion

| ------ | ---------- | ------------------- |

| m      | metre      | $1m \times 10^{0}$          |
| cm     | centimetre | $1m \times 10^{-2}$          |
| mm     | millimetre | $1m \times 10^{-3}$          |
| μm     | micrometre | $1m \times 10^{-6}$          |
| nm     | nanometre  | $1m \times 10^{-9}$          |

## Conversions of Volume
$$
\begin{flalign}
\\& dm^3 = l
\\& cm^3 = ml
\\& \frac{cm^3}{1000} = dm^3
\end{flalign}
$$
